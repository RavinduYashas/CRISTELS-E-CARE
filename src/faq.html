Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up with CRISTELS E-CARE?

You can visit our website and simply register using the "Login" button by entering your details in the registration section.

How can I channel a doctor?

Visit our website, login to your account, navigate to the E-Channeling section, select your preferred doctor, and click the "Select" button to view available slots.

How do I know if my appointment was successful?

Once you book an appointment, you will receive a confirmation via email through our automated system.

Who should I contact for inquiries?

You can reach us through our Contact Us page or submit an inquiry via the Support Desk for technical or medical assistance.
